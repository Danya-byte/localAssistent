# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect contributors to keep discussions respectful, technical, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Communicating respectfully and assuming good intent.
- Giving actionable technical feedback instead of personal criticism.
- Respecting different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Focusing discussions on the product, code, and user impact.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated bad-faith arguing or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Personal attacks in issues, pull requests, discussions, or chat.

## Scope

This policy applies to project spaces and to public interactions that are clearly tied to the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately through the security/contact channel listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
